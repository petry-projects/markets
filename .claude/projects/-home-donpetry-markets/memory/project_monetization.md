Monetization strategy: charge Market Managers for advanced features.

**Why:** Market managers are the operational hub — they derive the most operational value and have budget authority for market improvement tools.

**How to apply:** MVP is free for all roles. Post-MVP advanced manager features (analytics, broadcast tools, reliability dashboards) become the revenue layer. Product decisions should keep the manager value proposition strong.
